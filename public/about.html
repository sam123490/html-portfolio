Love

Their limbs are interlocked
The roots of a tree are coiled
Their bond grows stronger over time
The thick tree is harder to cut down
Their humor relieves the nearby
The tree shades those close to it
Their longevity blossoms with equilibrium
The tree survives with its deep roots
Their respect for each other falters briefly
The tree flowers after the harsh winter
They reach for their unforgotten dreams
The tree stretches to catch the sky
Their affection stumbles shortly
The tree survives a dry spell
Their strength comes from understanding
The tree never gives up on life

by Samuel Rodriguez
